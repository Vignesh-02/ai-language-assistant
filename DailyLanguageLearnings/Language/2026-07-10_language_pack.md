# Daily Language Pack (2026-07-10)

## Spanish
Words:
- puerta — door
- noche — night
- café — coffee
Phrases:
- abrir la puerta — open the door
- buenas noches — good night
- tomar café caliente — drink hot coffee
Sentences:
- La puerta está cerrada durante la noche en mi casa. — The door is closed during the night at my house.
- Tomo un café caliente cada noche antes de dormir. — I drink hot coffee every night before sleeping.
- Abro la puerta y saludo a mi amigo con una sonrisa. — I open the door and greet my friend with a smile.
