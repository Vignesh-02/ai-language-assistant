# Daily Language Pack (2026-07-06)

## Spanish
Words:
- piedra — stone
- bosque — forest
- río — river
Phrases:
- cruzar el río — cross the river
- caminar por el bosque — walk through the forest
- junto a la piedra — next to the stone
Sentences:
- El río corre rápido cerca del bosque verde. — The river runs quickly near the green forest.
- Cruzamos el río y encontramos una piedra grande. — We crossed the river and found a large stone.
- Caminamos por el bosque junto a la piedra ancient. — We walk through the forest next to the ancient stone.
