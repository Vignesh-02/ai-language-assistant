# Daily Language Pack (2026-06-24)

## Spanish
Words:
- libro — book
- café — coffee
- mano — hand
Phrases:
- leer un libro — to read a book
- tomar café caliente — to drink hot coffee
- levantar la mano — to raise one's hand
Sentences:
- Necesito un libro para leer durante el viaje esta tarde. — I need a book to read during the trip this afternoon.
- Mi mano sostiene la taza de café con cuidado. — My hand holds the cup of coffee carefully.
- Ella prefiere leer un libro mientras bebe café por la mañana. — She prefers to read a book while drinking coffee in the morning.
