# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-27)

## Spanish
Words:
- libro — book
- agua — water
- gato — cat
Phrases:
- Leer un libro — To read a book
- Beber agua fría — To drink cold water
- Un gato negro — A black cat
Sentences:
- Leo un libro interesante mientras bebo agua tranquilamente. — I read an interesting book while I drink water peacefully.
- Mi gato duerme cerca del libro que dejé en la mesa. — My cat sleeps near the book that I left on the table.
- El agua y el libro son mis compañeros en las tardes tranquilas. — Water and the book are my companions on quiet afternoons.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- pão — bread
- café — coffee
- casa — house
Phrases:
- Café com pão — Coffee with bread
- Volta para casa — Come back home
- Pão fresco — Fresh bread
Sentences:
- Tomo café com pão fresco todas as manhãs em casa. — I have coffee with fresh bread every morning at home.
- O pão e o café são essenciais quando volto para casa. — Bread and coffee are essential when I come back home.
- Na minha casa, preparo café e compro pão todos os dias. — At my house, I make coffee and buy bread every day.

## Italian
Words:
- porta — door
- finestra — window
- luce — light
Phrases:
- Aprire la porta — To open the door
- Dalla finestra — From the window
- Accendere la luce — To turn on the light
Sentences:
- Apro la porta e accendo la luce della casa subito. — I open the door and turn on the house light right away.
- Dalla finestra vedo la luce del tramonto che illumina tutto. — From the window I see the sunset light that illuminates everything.
- La porta e la finestra lasciano entrare la luce naturale. — The door and window let natural light come inside.

## French
Words:
- pain — bread
- lait — milk
- table — table
Phrases:
- Pain et lait — Bread and milk
- Sur la table — On the table
- Pain frais — Fresh bread
Sentences:
- Je mets du pain frais et du lait sur la table. — I put fresh bread and milk on the table.
- Le pain et le lait sont mes aliments préférés à table. — Bread and milk are my favorite foods at the table.
- Sur la table, je prépare du pain avec un verre de lait. — On the table, I prepare bread with a glass of milk.
