# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-17)

## Spanish
Words:
- sueño — sleep
- mesa — table
- mercado — market
Phrases:
- Tengo mucho sueño — I am very sleepy
- En el mercado — At the market
- Sobre la mesa — On the table
Sentences:
- Tengo sueño después de trabajar en el mercado todo el día. — I am sleepy after working at the market all day.
- Las frutas frescas están sobre la mesa del mercado. — The fresh fruits are on the market table.
- Mi sueño es vender productos buenos en el mercado. — My dream is to sell good products at the market.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- café — coffee
- cozinha — kitchen
- música — music
Phrases:
- Café quente na cozinha — Hot coffee in the kitchen
- Ouvir música agora — Listen to music now
- Na cozinha pela manhã — In the kitchen in the morning
Sentences:
- Bebo café quente na cozinha todas as manhãs. — I drink hot coffee in the kitchen every morning.
- A música toca enquanto preparo café na cozinha. — Music plays while I prepare coffee in the kitchen.
- Gosto de ouvir música e beber café quentinho. — I like to listen to music and drink hot coffee.

## Italian
Words:
- luce — light
- sera — evening
- prato — plate
Phrases:
- Nella sera — In the evening
- La luce è bella — The light is beautiful
- Un prato pulito — A clean plate
Sentences:
- Nella sera, la luce brilla dolcemente sulla tavola. — In the evening, the light shines sweetly on the table.
- Metto il cibo nel prato con luce bellissima. — I put the food on the plate with beautiful light.
- La luce della sera illumina il prato durante la cena. — The evening light illuminates the plate during dinner.

## French
Words:
- chambre — room
- lampe — lamp
- doux — sweet
Phrases:
- Dans la chambre — In the room
- La lampe brille — The lamp shines
- Goût doux et sucré — Sweet and sugary taste
Sentences:
- Je lis dans ma chambre avec la lampe allumée. — I read in my room with the lamp on.
- La lampe dans la chambre crée une lumière douce. — The lamp in the room creates a sweet light.
- Je prépare un goût doux dans la chambre avec la lampe. — I prepare a sweet taste in the room with the lamp.
