# Daily Language Pack (2026-05-10)

## Spanish
Words:
- mesa — table
- silla — chair
- pan — bread
Phrases:
- sentarse en la silla — to sit in the chair
- comer pan fresco — to eat fresh bread
- en la mesa de madera — on the wooden table
Sentences:
- La mesa está en el comedor con cuatro sillas alrededor. — The table is in the dining room with four chairs around it.
- Pongo pan fresco sobre la mesa cada mañana. — I put fresh bread on the table every morning.
- Me siento en la silla y disfruto comiendo pan con mantequilla. — I sit in the chair and enjoy eating bread with butter.
