# Daily Language Pack (2026-04-19)

## Spanish
Words:
- pan — bread
- leche — milk
- calle — street
Phrases:
- comprar pan fresco — buy fresh bread
- vaso de leche — glass of milk
- caminar por la calle — walk down the street
Sentences:
- Compré pan y leche en la tienda de la calle principal. — I bought bread and milk at the store on the main street.
- El pan está delicioso con un vaso de leche fría. — The bread is delicious with a glass of cold milk.
- Caminamos por la calle llevando pan y leche del mercado. — We walked down the street carrying bread and milk from the market.
