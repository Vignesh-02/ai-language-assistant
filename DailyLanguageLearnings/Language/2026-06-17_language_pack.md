# Daily Language Pack (2026-06-17)

## Spanish
Words:
- pan — bread
- leche — milk
- verdura — vegetable
Phrases:
- comprar pan fresco — buy fresh bread
- beber leche fría — drink cold milk
- comer verduras saludables — eat healthy vegetables
Sentences:
- Compré pan y leche en el mercado esta mañana temprano. — I bought bread and milk at the market early this morning.
- Mi hermana prefiere comer verduras con pan integral todos los días. — My sister prefers to eat vegetables with whole wheat bread every day.
- La leche fresca y el pan recién horneado son deliciosos juntos. — Fresh milk and freshly baked bread are delicious together.
