# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-29)

## Spanish
Words:
- puerta — door
- ventana — window
- mesa — table
Phrases:
- Abrir la puerta — to open the door
- Mirar por la ventana — to look through the window
- Sentarse en la mesa — to sit at the table
Sentences:
- La puerta de mi casa está cerca de la ventana grande. — The door of my house is near the big window.
- Coloco los platos en la mesa y abro la puerta para recibir visitas. — I place the plates on the table and open the door to receive visitors.
- Desde la ventana veo la mesa del jardín donde descansamos. — From the window I see the garden table where we rest.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- porta — door
- janela — window
- cadeira — chair
Phrases:
- Abrir a porta — to open the door
- Olhar pela janela — to look through the window
- Sentar na cadeira — to sit in the chair
Sentences:
- A porta e a janela da minha casa deixam entrar muita luz. — The door and window of my house let in a lot of light.
- Sento na cadeira e observo a porta aberta para o jardim. — I sit in the chair and observe the open door to the garden.
- Pela janela vejo a porta vermelha e uma cadeira embaixo da árvore. — Through the window I see the red door and a chair under the tree.

## Italian
Words:
- libro — book
- sedia — chair
- acqua — water
Phrases:
- Leggere un libro — to read a book
- Bere acqua fresca — to drink fresh water
- Sedere sulla sedia — to sit on the chair
Sentences:
- Leggo un libro mentre bevo acqua seduto sulla sedia comoda. — I read a book while drinking water sitting on the comfortable chair.
- La sedia e il libro sono i miei compagni quando bevo acqua fresca. — The chair and the book are my companions when I drink fresh water.
- Sulla sedia tranquilla, tengo il libro e l'acqua accanto a me. — On the quiet chair, I hold the book and water beside me.

## French
Words:
- chaise — chair
- verre — glass
- livre — book
Phrases:
- Lire un livre — to read a book
- Un verre d'eau — a glass of water
- S'asseoir sur la chaise — to sit on the chair
Sentences:
- Je m'assieds sur la chaise avec mon livre et un verre d'eau. — I sit on the chair with my book and a glass of water.
- La chaise, le livre et le verre sont ensemble sur ma terrasse. — The chair, the book, and the glass are together on my terrace.
- Sur la chaise confortable, je lis mon livre en buvant dans le verre. — On the comfortable chair, I read my book while drinking from the glass.
