# Daily Language Pack (2026-05-16)

## Spanish
Words:
- libro — book
- café — coffee
- noche — night
Phrases:
- leer un libro — to read a book
- tomar café caliente — to drink hot coffee
- buenas noches — good night
Sentences:
- Leo mi libro favorito con una taza de café cada noche. — I read my favorite book with a cup of coffee every night.
- El café está caliente y el libro es muy interesante para leer. — The coffee is hot and the book is very interesting to read.
- Cada noche bebo café antes de leer mi libro preferido. — Every night I drink coffee before reading my favorite book.
