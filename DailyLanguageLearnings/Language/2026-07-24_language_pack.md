# Daily Language Pack (2026-07-24)

## Spanish
Words:
- flor — flower
- gato — cat
- café — coffee
Phrases:
- tomar un café — to have a coffee
- acariciar un gato — to pet a cat
- oler una flor — to smell a flower
Sentences:
- El gato negro bebe leche en la cocina cada mañana. — The black cat drinks milk in the kitchen every morning.
- Mi hermano cultiva flores hermosas en su pequeño huerto. — My brother grows beautiful flowers in his small garden.
- Desayuno con café caliente y pan tostado los domingos. — I have breakfast with hot coffee and toast on Sundays.
