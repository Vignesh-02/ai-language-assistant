# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-16)

## Spanish
Words:
- jardín — garden
- amistad — friendship
- regalo — gift
Phrases:
- En el jardín florido — In the flowery garden
- Regalo especial para ti — Special gift for you
- Amistad verdadera y fuerte — True and strong friendship
Sentences:
- El jardín es hermoso con flores de muchos colores diferentes. — The garden is beautiful with flowers of many different colors.
- Mi amistad con él es valiosa y dura toda la vida. — My friendship with him is valuable and lasts a lifetime.
- Le doy un regalo especial en el jardín bajo el sol. — I give him a special gift in the garden under the sun.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- sorrir — to smile
- coração — heart
- espelho — mirror
Phrases:
- Sorrir de verdade — To smile genuinely
- Com o coração aberto — With an open heart
- Vejo no espelho — I see in the mirror
Sentences:
- Sorrio quando vejo meu amigo chegar com alegria. — I smile when I see my friend arrive with joy.
- Meu coração sorri com puro contentamento todos os dias. — My heart smiles with pure contentment every day.
- Olho no espelho e sorrio para mim mesmo com esperança. — I look in the mirror and smile at myself with hope.

## Italian
Words:
- speranza — hope
- sorriso — smile
- cuore — heart
Phrases:
- Pieno di speranza — Full of hope
- Con un sorriso dolce — With a sweet smile
- Nel cuore profondo — In the deep heart
Sentences:
- La speranza vive nel cuore di chi sorride ogni giorno. — Hope lives in the heart of one who smiles every day.

- Il sorriso sincero viene dal cuore pieno di speranza vera. — The sincere smile comes from a heart full of true hope.

## French
Words:
- sourire — smile
- cœur — heart
- espoir — hope
Phrases:
- Sourire du cœur — Smile from the heart
- Plein d'espoir — Full of hope
- Le cœur content — The happy heart
Sentences:
- Je souris avec mon cœur rempli d'espoir chaque matin. — I smile with my heart full of hope every morning.
- L'espoir dans le cœur me fait sourire face aux défis. — Hope in the heart makes me smile in the face of challenges.
- Mon cœur sourit avec espoir quand je vois la beauté du monde. — My heart smiles with hope when I see the beauty of the world.
