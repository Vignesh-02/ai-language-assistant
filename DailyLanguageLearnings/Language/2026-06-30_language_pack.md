# Daily Language Pack (2026-06-30)

## Spanish
Words:
- agua — water
- flor — flower
- coser — to sew
Phrases:
- beber agua fresca — to drink fresh water
- flores en el jardín — flowers in the garden
- aguja para coser — needle for sewing
Sentences:
- El agua fresca es esencial para beber durante el día. — Fresh water is essential to drink during the day.
- Las flores coloridas crecen bien con agua abundante. — Colorful flowers grow well with plenty of water.
- Mi madre usa aguja para coser con cuidado. — My mother uses a needle to sew carefully.
