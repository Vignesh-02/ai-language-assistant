# Daily Language Pack (2026-06-08)

## Spanish
Words:
- libro — book
- gato — cat
- café — coffee
Phrases:
- tomar un café — to have a coffee
- leer un libro — to read a book
- acariciar al gato — to pet the cat
Sentences:
- Leo un libro mientras bebo mi café por la mañana. — I read a book while drinking my coffee in the morning.
- Mi gato duerme cerca de mi libro favorito cada día. — My cat sleeps near my favorite book every day.
- Tomo café y leo mientras mi gato descansa tranquilo. — I have coffee and read while my cat rests peacefully.
