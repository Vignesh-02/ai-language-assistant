# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-22)

## Spanish
Words:
- mano — hand
- tiempo — time
- corazón — heart
Phrases:
- De la mano — hand in hand
- Tengo tiempo — I have time
- Con todo el corazón — with all my heart
Sentences:
- Camino de la mano con mi hermana por el parque. — I walk hand in hand with my sister through the park.
- El tiempo pasa rápido cuando estoy con las personas que amo. — Time passes quickly when I am with the people I love.
- Mi corazón está lleno de alegría y amo el tiempo con mi familia. — My heart is full of joy and I love time with my family.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- mão — hand
- tempo — time
- livro — book
Phrases:
- De mão dada — hand in hand
- Tenho pouco tempo — I have little time
- Ler um livro — to read a book
Sentences:
- Ando de mão dada com minha mãe no parque todos os dias. — I walk hand in hand with my mother in the park every day.
- Leio um livro quando tenho tempo livre à noite. — I read a book when I have free time at night.
- Meu tempo e minha mão estão dedicados a ler livros interessantes. — My time and my hand are dedicated to reading interesting books.

## Italian
Words:
- mano — hand
- tempo — time
- mare — sea
Phrases:
- Mano nella mano — hand in hand
- Con il tempo — in time
- Vicino al mare — near the sea
Sentences:
- Cammino mano nella mano con il mio amico sulla spiaggia. — I walk hand in hand with my friend on the beach.
- Con il tempo, imparerò a nuotare nel mare blu. — In time, I will learn to swim in the blue sea.
- La mano del tempo passa lentamente vicino al mare tranquillo. — The hand of time passes slowly near the calm sea.

## French
Words:
- main — hand
- temps — time
- jardin — garden
Phrases:
- Main dans la main — hand in hand
- Avec le temps — with time
- Dans le jardin — in the garden
Sentences:
- Je marche main dans la main avec mon ami dans le jardin. — I walk hand in hand with my friend in the garden.
- Avec le temps, je cultive des fleurs dans mon jardin. — With time, I cultivate flowers in my garden.
- La main du temps change tout dans le jardin lentement. — The hand of time changes everything in the garden slowly.
