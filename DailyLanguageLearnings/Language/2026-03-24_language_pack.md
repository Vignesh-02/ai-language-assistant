# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-24)

## Spanish
Words:
- noche — night
- música — music
- calle — street
Phrases:
- Por la noche — In the night
- Escuchar música — Listen to music
- En la calle — On the street
Sentences:
- Escucho música en la calle por la noche. — I listen to music on the street at night.
- La música suena hermosa en la noche oscura. — The music sounds beautiful in the dark night.
- Por la noche, camino por la calle escuchando música. — At night, I walk down the street listening to music.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- noite — night
- rua — street
- flor — flower
Phrases:
- À noite — At night
- Na rua — On the street
- Uma flor bonita — A beautiful flower
Sentences:
- À noite, vejo flores na rua quando passo. — At night, I see flowers on the street when I pass.
- Na rua há uma flor linda que brilha à noite. — On the street there is a beautiful flower that shines at night.
- Colho uma flor na rua durante a noite estrelada. — I pick a flower on the street during the starry night.

## Italian
Words:
- strada — street
- cuore — heart
- sole — sun
Phrases:
- In strada — On the street
- Con il cuore — With the heart
- Al sole — In the sun
Sentences:
- Cammino in strada con il cuore pieno di gioia. — I walk on the street with a heart full of joy.
- Il sole brilla sulla strada dove cammino con il cuore allegro. — The sun shines on the street where I walk with a happy heart.
- In strada al sole, sento il cuore battere forte. — On the street in the sun, I feel my heart beat strongly.

## French
Words:
- cœur — heart
- rue — street
- soleil — sun
Phrases:
- Dans la rue — On the street
- De tout cœur — Wholeheartedly
- Au soleil — In the sun
Sentences:
- Je marche dans la rue au soleil de tout cœur. — I walk down the street in the sun wholeheartedly.
- Mon cœur danse dans la rue quand le soleil brille. — My heart dances on the street when the sun shines.
- De tout cœur, je profite du soleil dans la rue. — Wholeheartedly, I enjoy the sun on the street.
