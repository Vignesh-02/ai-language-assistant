# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-20)

## Spanish
Words:
- ventana — window
- libro — book
- agua — water
Phrases:
- Por la ventana — Through the window
- Leer un libro — To read a book
- Un vaso de agua — A glass of water
Sentences:
- Miro por la ventana y veo un libro sobre la mesa. — I look through the window and see a book on the table.
- Bebo agua mientras leo un libro en la ventana. — I drink water while reading a book by the window.
- El libro está cerca de la ventana con un vaso de agua. — The book is near the window with a glass of water.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- porta — door
- sol — sun
- amigo — friend
Phrases:
- Abrir a porta — To open the door
- Dia de sol — A sunny day
- Meu amigo querido — My dear friend
Sentences:
- Abro a porta quando meu amigo chega no dia de sol. — I open the door when my friend arrives on a sunny day.
- Meu amigo entra pela porta com o sol brilhando. — My friend enters through the door with the sun shining.
- A porta está aberta para meu amigo no dia ensolarado. — The door is open for my friend on the sunny day.

## Italian
Words:
- finestra — window
- stella — star
- notte — night
Phrases:
- Dalla finestra — From the window
- Una stella brillante — A bright star
- Nella notte profonda — In the deep night
Sentences:
- Guardo dalla finestra una stella nella notte buia. — I look from the window at a star in the dark night.
- Una stella brilla dalla finestra durante la notte. — A star shines from the window during the night.
- Nella notte, vedo una stella brillante dalla finestra. — In the night, I see a bright star from the window.

## French
Words:
- arbre — tree
- fleur — flower
- ami — friend
Phrases:
- Sous l'arbre — Under the tree
- Une belle fleur — A beautiful flower
- Mon ami cher — My dear friend
Sentences:
- Je vois mon ami sous l'arbre avec une belle fleur. — I see my friend under the tree with a beautiful flower.
- Mon ami cueille une fleur sous l'arbre verdoyant. — My friend picks a flower under the green tree.
- Sous l'arbre, mon ami admire la fleur colorée. — Under the tree, my friend admires the colorful flower.
