# Daily Language Pack (2026-05-26)

## Spanish
Words:
- ventana — window
- silla — chair
- mesa — table
Phrases:
- cerca de la ventana — near the window
- sentarse en la silla — to sit in the chair
- limpiar la mesa — to clean the table
Sentences:
- La ventana está abierta y la silla es cómoda. — The window is open and the chair is comfortable.
- Me siento en la silla cerca de la ventana cada tarde. — I sit in the chair near the window every afternoon.
- La mesa y la silla son de madera en mi cocina. — The table and chair are made of wood in my kitchen.
