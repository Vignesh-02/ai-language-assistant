# Daily Language Pack (2026-07-07)

## Spanish
Words:
- ventana — window
- mesa — table
- silla — chair
Phrases:
- cerca de la ventana — near the window
- sentarse en la silla — to sit in the chair
- limpiar la mesa — to clean the table
Sentences:
- La ventana está abierta y la mesa está limpia hoy. — The window is open and the table is clean today.
- Me siento en la silla cerca de la ventana cada mañana. — I sit in the chair near the window every morning.
- La mesa y la silla son muebles importantes en la casa. — The table and chair are important furniture in the house.
