# Daily Language Pack (2026-05-18)

## Spanish
Words:
- mesa — table
- silla — chair
- comida — food
Phrases:
- sentarse en la silla — to sit in the chair
- comer en la mesa — to eat at the table
- comida deliciosa — delicious food
Sentences:
- La silla está junto a la mesa en el comedor. — The chair is next to the table in the dining room.
- Preparo comida sabrosa en la mesa cada día. — I prepare tasty food on the table every day.
- Me siento en la silla para comer comida caliente. — I sit in the chair to eat hot food.
