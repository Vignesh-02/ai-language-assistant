# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-14)

## Spanish
Words:
- pan — bread
- gato — cat
- ropa — clothing
Phrases:
- Tengo sed — I am thirsty
- Pan fresco hoy — Fresh bread today
- El gato duerme — The cat sleeps
Sentences:
- Mi gato come pan en la mesa cada mañana. — My cat eats bread on the table every morning.
- Tengo sed y quiero pan con mantequilla ahora. — I am thirsty and want bread with butter now.
- La ropa del gato está limpia y bonita. — The cat's clothing is clean and beautiful.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- peixe — fish
- leite — milk
- queijo — cheese
Phrases:
- Gosto de peixe fresco — I like fresh fish
- Beber leite quente — Drink hot milk
- Queijo delicioso aqui — Delicious cheese here
Sentences:
- Bebo leite morno toda manhã com queijo e pão. — I drink warm milk every morning with cheese and bread.
- O peixe fresco é delicioso com leite e alho. — The fresh fish is delicious with milk and garlic.
- Meu amigo gosta de queijo e peixe no almoço. — My friend likes cheese and fish for lunch.

## Italian
Words:
- vino — wine
- caro — dear
- dolce — sweet
Phrases:
- Vino rosso buono — Good red wine
- Caro amico mio — My dear friend
- Dolce di casa — Homemade sweet
Sentences:
- Bevo vino con il mio caro amico nella stanza. — I drink wine with my dear friend in the room.
- Il dolce è delizioso e il vino è caro. — The sweet is delicious and the wine is expensive.
- Mio caro, questo vino dolce è meraviglioso stasera. — My dear, this sweet wine is wonderful tonight.

## French
Words:
- vin — wine
- sucre — sugar
- froid — cold
Phrases:
- Vin blanc frais — Fresh white wine
- Sucre dans le café — Sugar in the coffee
- Jour froid aujourd'hui — Cold day today
Sentences:
- Je bois du vin froid avec du sucre et du pain. — I drink cold wine with sugar and bread.
- Mon ami aime le vin blanc sucré les jours froids. — My friend likes sweet white wine on cold days.
- Aujourd'hui il fait froid et je veux du vin chaud. — Today it is cold and I want hot wine.
