# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-11)

## Spanish
Words:
- silla — chair
- comida — food
- hermano — brother
Phrases:
- ¿Dónde está la silla? — Where is the chair?
- Me encanta esta comida — I love this food
- Mi hermano es amable — My brother is kind
Sentences:
- Mi hermano se sienta en la silla para comer comida. — My brother sits in the chair to eat food.
- La silla azul está cerca de la comida en la mesa. — The blue chair is near the food on the table.
- Mi hermano prepara comida deliciosa en la casa. — My brother prepares delicious food at home.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- chuva — rain
- amigo — friend
- carro — car
Phrases:
- Que chuva forte! — What heavy rain!
- Meu amigo chegou — My friend arrived
- O carro está pronto — The car is ready
Sentences:
- Meu amigo chegou de carro durante a chuva forte. — My friend arrived by car during heavy rain.
- O carro do meu amigo parou por causa da chuva. — My friend's car stopped because of the rain.
- Espero meu amigo no carro quando chove muito. — I wait for my friend in the car when it rains a lot.

## Italian
Words:
- musica — music
- stanza — room
- finestra — window
Phrases:
- Ascolta la musica — Listen to the music
- In questa stanza — In this room
- Dalla finestra vedo — From the window I see
Sentences:
- Ascolto la musica nella stanza con la finestra aperta. — I listen to music in the room with the open window.
- La musica viene dalla stanza accanto alla finestra grande. — The music comes from the room next to the big window.
- Mi piace suonare musica nella mia stanza con la finestra. — I like to play music in my room with the window.

## French
Words:
- chat — cat
- livre — book
- cuisine — kitchen
Phrases:
- Le chat noir — The black cat
- Lire un livre — To read a book
- Dans la cuisine — In the kitchen
Sentences:
- Le chat dort dans la cuisine près du livre ouvert. — The cat sleeps in the kitchen near the open book.
- Je lis mon livre dans la cuisine avec mon chat. — I read my book in the kitchen with my cat.
- Le chat regarde mon livre pendant que je cuisine. — The cat watches my book while I cook.
