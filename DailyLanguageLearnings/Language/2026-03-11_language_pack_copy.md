# Daily Language Pack (2026-03-11)

## Spanish
Words:
- puerta — door
- tiempo — time/weather
- noche — night
Phrases:
- Buenas noches — Good evening/night
- ¿Qué hora es? — What time is it?
- Hasta mañana — See you tomorrow
Sentences:
- La puerta está abierta durante el tiempo de visita. — The door is open during visiting time.
- ¿Tienes tiempo esta noche para hablar conmigo? — Do you have time tonight to talk with me?
- Cierro la puerta antes de la noche cada día. — I close the door before night every day.

## Brazilian Portuguese
Words:
- livro — book
- trabalho — work
- coração — heart
Phrases:
- Com todo o coração — With all my heart
- Eu adoro ler — I love to read
- Que dia é hoje? — What day is today?
Sentences:
- Meu livro favorito fala sobre trabalho e dedicação. — My favorite book talks about work and dedication.
- Faço este trabalho com todo o coração e alma. — I do this work with all my heart and soul.
- Comprei um livro novo para estudar no trabalho. — I bought a new book to study at work.

## Italian
Words:
- gatto — cat
- amico — friend
- cibo — food
Phrases:
- Mi piace molto — I like it very much
- Grazie mille — Thank you very much
- Per favore, aiutami — Please, help me
Sentences:
- Il mio gatto mangia il cibo ogni mattina con entusiasmo. — My cat eats food every morning with enthusiasm.
- Un amico vero condivide il cibo e il tempo con te. — A true friend shares food and time with you.
- Mi piace molto stare con il mio gatto e il mio amico. — I like very much to spend time with my cat and my friend.

## French
Words:
- enfant — child
- école — school
- ami — friend
Phrases:
- À bientôt — See you soon
- C'est magnifique — It's magnificent
- Je suis heureux — I am happy
Sentences:
- L'enfant va à l'école avec son meilleur ami chaque jour. — The child goes to school with his best friend every day.
- Mon ami est un enfant heureux qui aime l'école. — My friend is a happy child who loves school.
- À l'école, cet enfant et cet ami jouent ensemble. — At school, this child and this friend play together.
